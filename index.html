A cigarette, smoke, or tobacco roll is a product made of tobacco leaves, rolled or stuffed into a cylinder-shaped paper sleeve, usually 120 mm long and 10 mm in diameter. Some countries that manufacture cigarettes allow cigarettes to be made not only from tobacco leaves but even from the stem of the plant, which is crushed and cut. The cigarette is lit from one end, and the other end is placed in the mouth.
